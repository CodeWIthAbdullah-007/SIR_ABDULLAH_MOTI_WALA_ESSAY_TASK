Technology in Our Daily Life

Introduction

Technology has become an important part of our lives. It helps us communicate, study, work, and enjoy entertainment.

Today, almost everyone uses a smartphone or a computer for different purposes.

Benefits of Technology

  • Improves communication
  • Makes learning easier
  • Saves time
  • Provides online services

Disadvantages of Technology

  1. Can cause addiction
  2. Privacy concerns
  3. Health issues due to excessive screen time
